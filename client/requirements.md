## Packages
@mediapipe/tasks-vision | Real-time face and gaze tracking via webcam
framer-motion | Dynamic Island animations and smooth UI transitions
recharts | Telemetry data visualization and session analytics
date-fns | Date formatting and duration calculations

## Notes
- MediaPipe WASM files are loaded via CDN in the frontend hook to prevent Vite bundling issues with WASM.
- The app operates in a forced dark-mode to emulate a premium macOS utility aesthetic.
